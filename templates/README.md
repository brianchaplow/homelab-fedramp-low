# FedRAMP Templates

Official templates from fedramp.gov, checked into this repo for reproducibility.
Each artifact in `poam/`, `inventory/`, and `deviation-requests/` is generated
by populating the corresponding template.

## Sources

- POA&M: https://www.fedramp.gov/resources/templates/FedRAMP-POAM-Template.xlsx
- IIW (SSP App M): https://www.fedramp.gov/resources/templates/SSP-Appendix-M-Integrated-Inventory-Workbook-Template.xlsx
- DR Form (Vulnerability): https://www.fedramp.gov/resources/templates/FedRAMP-Vulnerability-Deviation-Request-Form.xlsx

Downloaded on: 2026-04-08

## Revision

fedramp.gov serves these from `/resources/templates/` without a rev marker in
the path. They are categorized under the Rev 5 templates page
(`https://www.fedramp.gov/rev5/documents-templates/`) and are the current
production versions as of download date.

- POA&M Template:            Rev 5
- IIW Template:              Rev 5
- DR Form (Vulnerability):   Rev 5
