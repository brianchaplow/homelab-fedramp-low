### This System

Not applicable. MSS is a single-operator personal homelab system. The System Owner (Brian Chaplow) is the only personnel role associated with this system. Formal anti-counterfeit training delivery -- requiring a curriculum, training records, and distinct trainer/trainee roles -- is not applicable to a one-person system.

Anti-counterfeit awareness is embedded in the operator's procurement and installation practices: direct-from-vendor hardware purchasing (no gray-market), official-channel software installation, and apt GPG key verification at install time. The System Owner's professional background (27 years military service, active cybersecurity ConMon program development) provides the awareness of supply chain risk and counterfeit indicators that a formal training program would otherwise deliver.
