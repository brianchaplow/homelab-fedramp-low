# sc-28.1 - \[System and Communications Protection\] Cryptographic Protection

## Control Statement

Implement cryptographic mechanisms to prevent unauthorized disclosure and modification of the following information at rest on [system components or media]: [information].

## Control Assessment Objective

- \[SC-28(01)[01]\] cryptographic mechanisms are implemented to prevent unauthorized disclosure of [information] at rest on [system components or media];

- \[SC-28(01)[02]\] cryptographic mechanisms are implemented to prevent unauthorized modification of [information] at rest on [system components or media].

## Control guidance

The selection of cryptographic mechanisms is based on the need to protect the confidentiality and integrity of organizational information. The strength of mechanism is commensurate with the security category or classification of the information. Organizations have the flexibility to encrypt information on system components or media or encrypt data structures, including files, records, or fields.

### This System

Cryptographic protection of information at rest is not currently implemented for the MSS boundary. No full-disk encryption (FDE) is applied to any in-boundary host's boot or data drives -- the haccp Samsung 990 EVO Plus 2TB PCAP drive (`/dev/nvme0n1`, mounted at `/opt/arkime/raw`) holds the highest-sensitivity data (full-PCAP archives) and is unencrypted. PBS backup snapshots on smokehouse NFS are stored without PBS datastore-level encryption. Docker volumes holding Wazuh alert indices, OpenCTI threat intelligence data, and ELK log indices are filesystem-protected but not cryptographically encrypted. Physical security of the home rack is the sole compensating control at this time.

The planned remediation for this control follows a three-step path: (1) enable PBS datastore encryption for backup jobs at the Proxmox Backup Server CE datastore level, which supports AES-256 encryption with a passphrase or key file; (2) deploy LUKS full-disk encryption on haccp's PCAP drive (`/dev/nvme0n1`) given the sensitivity of 2TB of full-PCAP data -- this would require a one-time reformat and restore from Arkime re-capture; (3) evaluate Wazuh Indexer and OpenCTI Docker volume encryption options. These remediations are deferred to a future hardening phase. The upgrade path does not overlap with `runbooks/cert-trust.md`, which covers transmission TLS only and does not address at-rest encryption.

#### Implementation Status: planned
